Profile Video 2020
Since Facebook introduced the feature allowing the use of a profile video as opposed to a profile picture, I’ve had a lot of fun creating these short videos starring myself.

For this 2020 edition, I had the idea of filming myself holding the tape cover of my favorite VHS camera in mugshot fashion (like you see in the movies). While working on the edit, I took it one step further and circuit bent the footage which gives it that glitched look.

Music by Fosa YG
